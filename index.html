About Us

Our Story

Good Shepherd Land Co. was founded with a simple mission: to provide landowners with a fair, transparent, and hassle-free way to sell their vacant land. As a family-owned business based in Clarksville, TN, we understand the unique challenges that come with owning land—from rising property taxes to maintenance costs and uncertainty about the property's future value.

Whether you've inherited land you don't need, are facing financial difficulties, or simply want to liquidate a real estate asset quickly, we're here to help. We pride ourselves on treating every seller with respect and fairness, offering cash solutions that work for your timeline.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make selling land simple and stress-free. We believe in:

  • Fair Offers: We evaluate each property thoroughly to provide competitive cash offers
  • Transparency: No hidden fees, no commissions—what we offer is what you get
  • Speed: We can close on your timeline, whether that's 2 weeks or 2 months
  • Integrity: We treat every seller the way we'd want to be treated

The process is simple and secure:
  1. Accept the Offer: Once you accept our offer, we'll send you a purchase agreement to review and sign.
  2. Open Escrow: We open escrow with a reputable title company or real estate attorney.
  3. Title Search: The title company conducts a thorough title search, which typically takes 7-14 days.
  4. Close the Deal: If the title is clear, we proceed to closing. We send funds to the title company, you sign the closing documents (can often be done remotely), and they disburse your payment via wire transfer or cashier's check—your choice.

The entire process usually takes 30-45 days from start to finish, but we can work faster or slower based on your timeline.
